Introduction

I am an award-winning mathematics lecturer and former Head of Teaching & Learning at ATU Sligo, with over 20 years' experience in higher education. As a qualified science teacher, I specialize in inclusive, analytics-driven STEM education that empowers learners through mastery-based assessment, reflective feedback, and curiosity-led learning.

My work bridges theory and practice, focusing on inclusive STEM education approaches and digital transformation in education—supporting ATU's strategic goals in equity, digital transformation, and student success.
