Engaged Research: Bringing Mathematics to Life

As a qualified science teacher, I specialize in inclusive, analytics-driven STEM education that empowers learners through mastery-based assessment, reflective feedback, and curiosity-led learning.

My work spans mentoring, learning analytics, and AI-enhanced pedagogy—supporting ATU's strategic goals in equity, digital transformation, and student success.
